The Materials.

Our robot will have base layer of base layer of carbon steel surrounded by carbon fiber to make it bulletproof.

How Does It Work?

Our robot works though AI and pings. The soldier can use an app on his phone to ping the robot if he is able, but this wont always be the case. If this doesn't work the AI uses color and audio detection. It finds soldiers based on uniform color and decibel sound units. If the gunshots and screams come from the same direction the robot can put two and two together and travel to the source of the sound to help.

What Does It Hold?

The robot will hold supplies live IV fluids and tubing. The amount will depend on the length of mission. Normal Saline/Sodium Chloride, Hetastarch/Hextend, and Lactated Ringers(LR) are usually carried. 18, 16, and 14 gauge IV catheters. FAST 1 intraosseous infusion kit. The FAST 1 is a quick way to administer fluids when peripheral and external jugular venous access is unavailable due to massive blood loss, burns, or loss of limbs.

Who Can Buy It?

Since we are based in the United States of America, only the US military can buy it. The only exception is allies of the USA or the sides that America supports.

Are There Any Risks?

There are no worries, we promise. Our company can not stress this enough, this robot WILL NOT kill people, it just won't.
